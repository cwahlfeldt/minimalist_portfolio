About

My name is Chris Wahlfeldt. I’m a 3D artist and aspiring programmer based out of Champaign-Urbana Illinois. I’m passionate about all things computers but I sway towards Game/Web Development.

Other than my love computers, I am a spirited musician that loves playing shows. Before going to school and earning an associates degree in Digital Media, I was in multiple bands that toured across the country. Playing music and touring has given me a better understanding of the world and the people inhabiting it. It also introduced me to some of the best people I know and love.

I am currently working as a server at Blackdog Smoke and ale house for which I also created the website. I’m constantly keeping my self busy with side projects as well as taking courses for a computer science degree.
